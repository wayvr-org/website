### Quick Start Guide

1. **Start your VR environment**: Launch Monado, WiVRn, or SteamVR
2. **Run the overlay**: Execute WayVR with your preferred method
3. **Select your screens**: Choose which desktop screens to share in VR
4. **Start interacting**: Use your controllers to navigate your desktop in VR - **look at your left hand to get started!**
