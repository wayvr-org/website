## rectangle widget

### `<rectangle>`

### A styled rectangle

#### Parameters

`color`: #FFAABB | #FFAABBCC

_1st gradient color_

`color2`: #FFAABB | #FFAABBCC

_2nd gradient color_

`gradient`: "horizontal" | "vertical" | "radial" | "none"

`round`: **float** (default: 0) | **percent** (0-100%)

`border`: **float**

`border_color`: #FFAABB | #FFAABBCC
